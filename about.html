About Me

Hiring me means...

You're getting a developer who combines technical skills with a security-first mindset. I build applications that not only look great but are resilient against common vulnerabilities. My experience in both development and cybersecurity allows me to create solutions that are functional and secure.

Working with me means...

Collaborating with someone who values clean code, thorough documentation, and continuous improvement. I believe in transparent communication and actively seek feedback to deliver the best possible results. I thrive in team environments where knowledge sharing is encouraged.

My Background

I'm currently pursuing my Computer Programming diploma at Humber College while working part-time at WIS International. This balance has taught me valuable skills in time management, problem-solving under pressure, and effective communication in professional settings.

My technical journey includes mastering HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and exploring backend technologies. I'm particularly passionate about cybersecurity and aim to contribute to open-source security tools. Beyond coding, I enjoy studying ethical hacking techniques and understanding how to build more secure applications.

One of my core principles is that great developers never stop learning. I dedicate time each week to explore new technologies, experiment with different frameworks, and participate in coding challenges to sharpen my skills.
